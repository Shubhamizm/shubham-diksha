Our Story

Diksha and I met just 7 months ago via a matrimony website. At first, it was just a few text messages exchanged between two people living in different cities. But those texts soon turned into long calls, which then evolved into video calls. It was as if each conversation revealed more of how perfectly aligned we were, like two parts of a whole finally finding each other. Despite the physical distance, we felt incredibly close. After a month of these wonderful exchanges, I decided it was time to meet her in person. I flew to Mumbai, my heart filled with anticipation and a hint of nervousness. When I finally saw her, coming towards me with the warmest smile, everything felt just right. We spent the day exploring together—starting with a cozy café, then a peaceful beach walk, and ending with a night out at a club, where time seemed to stand still. In a sweet, spontaneous moment, we even bought each other sunflowers, a memory that still makes me smile. That first day together was everything I had hoped for and more. And in that moment, I knew—I was going to marry this girl!

Events

10th December

Sangeet 🎉💃🕺7PM - 10PM

Close friends and family members from both the bride's and groom's sides will showcase special dance performances, adding to the excitement of the evening. The highlight will be a captivating performance by the bride and groom themselves. After that, the stage will be open for everyone to join in, ensuring a night full of fun, laughter, and cherished memories.

11th December

Haldi 🌼💛🌿11AM onwards

The Haldi ceremony will be an intimate gathering of family and friends. Along with the traditional ritual of applying turmeric to the bride and groom for blessings and a natural glow, the event will feature surprise dance performances and entertaining games.

Wedding 👰🤵💞7PM onwards

The wedding ceremony will be the grand highlight, where the couple will tie the knot surrounded by everyone. The Baraat will depart at 6 PM, setting a lively tone for the evening. All guests are invited to join the celebration as the reception and dinner start at 7 PM. The sacred Pheras will take place at 1:00 AM in the presence of close ones, marking the moment the couple is declared husband and wife, beginning their beautiful journey together.

14th December

Aashirwad Samaroh 😇💐✨7PM - 11:30PM

We are honored to invite our esteemed guests to a heartfelt blessing ceremony, where they will bestow their blessings upon us as we embark on this beautiful journey together. Along with our friends and family, many guests from Jaipur and elsewhere who could not join us at the wedding will attend this special ceremony. The reception dinner will begin at 7 PM.

Venue - The Royal Palace, Jaipur.
