Our Story

Diksha and I met just 8 months ago via a matrimony website. At first, it was just a few text messages exchanged between two people living in different cities. But those texts soon turned into long calls, which then evolved into video calls. It was as if each conversation revealed more of how perfectly aligned we were, like two parts of a whole finally finding each other. Despite the physical distance, we felt incredibly close, our connection growing stronger with each passing day. After a month of these wonderful exchanges, I decided it was time to meet her in person. I flew to Mumbai, my heart filled with anticipation and a hint of nervousness. The moment I saw her, there with a smile, I knew. We met in cafe, then went to beach and then to club in the night. We bought sunflowers for each other. Our first meeting was everything I had hoped for and more. It was in that instant, I realized I was going to marry this girl!
